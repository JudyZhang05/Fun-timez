KLICKY (2022)

The Klicky Project is a team-based task developed, overseered, and made possible by the WIT in LinkedIn, Breakthrough Tech, and Magnit Global. The group consisted of 5 college undergraduates, including myself. We worked collaboratively to modify a LinkedIn clone website and track the user's interaction. Throughout the development of Klicky, we split into 2 different groups dedicated to various areas of the project. I was tasked with HTML/CSS and Firebase Database Team. In HTML/CSS, I utilized my knowledge in HTML/CSS and focused primarily on the presentation of the website, some tasks I completed were adding new sections to the website to diversify the interactions available to the user, manipulated the scale of individual sections, added posts to replicate an active platform, creating mouse effects for a more engaging experience, and more. In the Firebase Database team, I focused on collecting and keeping track of user's interaction with the webpage and adding them to a firebase database. When the user clicks on a button or anywhere on the screen, it would automatically append itself to the database instantly.

FEEL RELEAF (2023)

The Feel ReLeaf Project is a mental health awareness website where one is given the opportunity to relieve their deepest darkest secret anonymously or sign up to recieve professional help. This project seconds as my Code First Girls Javascript Kickstarter Course graduating project. The website includes a language translation option to be inclusive to people on a global scale. Additionally, the website switches between a typing section and reading section. The website's design is developed to emulate an open space/environment where you're able to as the title states `Feel Releaf (Relief)`. On the last day of the course, I presented the website in its final stage of development with working buttons and transitional functions. I concluded the presentation with a demo and graduated from the course with a Completion of the Introduction to Javascript certificate which could be found on my LinkedIn.

STELLAR (2024)

This website was the final project developed for Hunter College's MEDP 285 - Web Production I course. The project required me to design a wireframe with responsiveness and create a professionally oriented website. The topic I chose is a start-up company on stickers and scrapbooking material for all ages. The wide range of target audiences is purposely intended as a way to become the main source that people will flock to when they either need to restock their never-ending collection, start their scrapbooking journey, or simply gift stationery items for friends and family. This website will be presented on the web in a welcoming, friendly, and accessible way. Where categories have both their distinct borders and intertwine in an alluring way. Additionally, the products would be transparent with everything, from its manufacturer to its commissioned artist. The company aims to gain the full trust and complete assurance of its customers to ensure a successful user experience.
