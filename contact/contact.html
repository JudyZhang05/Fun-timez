Hello! I'm Judy. A little bit about me, I majored in fine arts and graduated from Laguardia with a chancellors endorsed diploma for the arts, and got the honor of recieving an ALUMNI AND FRIENDS of Laguardia The Milton Glaser Graphic Art Award. So I consider myself an art enthusiast and along the way I got really interested in graphic design, now my father works as an IT supporter so tech has been around me my whole life. I found intertwining the arts with tech captivating so to learn more about the tech side of things I enrolled in computer science courses and programs with the intentions to expand my career choices. Turns out, if you look at it the right way painting and coding are quite similar! Now I'm a Daedalus Scholar at Hunter College majoring in Media Studies and minoring in Computer Science. I look foward to working with you if I get the chance to. Thank you!
