STELLAR

Role

UI Designer & Front-End Developer

Tool

Figma, Github, Visual Studio

Duration

May 2024 - 1 week

Overview

Stellar is a website for a conceptual start-up company of stickers and scrapbooking stationery items for all ages. The aspiration is to combat the normalized and unethicality of stolen art by supporting original artists' works while offering affordable and high-quality products all in a user-centered and accessible design.

Information Architecture

I created a rather simple sitemap to illustrate how each page of the website would flow and connect with each other. This design was planned to have user's mainly navigate from a navigation bar fixed to the top of the page for the users' convenience.

Design

I knew I wanted the website to incorporate a mature but jolly look that would be easy and simple to navigate through. For this, I came up with an initial pastel, muted cool-toned color palette. Using this pallet, I then scoured common-use photography, ultimately selecting a calming photograph of a pleasant blue sky with clouds decorating the borders, creating an illusive frame.

Final Product
The final deliverable I published was a low-fidelity conceptual website holding placeholder content and imagery to reflect the potential start-up small-business brand identity.

NOTE: This site is no longer being hosted.
Reflecting Back

Looking back there are things I would have wanted to implement if this project were to be continued.

For a more intuitive user journey, adding a filter search feature will sort through genres, prices, colors, styles, and artists. For those who are just beginning their sticker collection or scrapbooking journey, an optional and quick survey to figure out what styles would best cater to their preferences.

Additionally, this project took place before I properly understood the design thinking process and the intricacies of developing a thoughtfully woven equity and accessibility-focused design mindset. That said, I acknowledge how I have heavily missed out on crucial steps in the design process such as defining a more realistic user audience, ideating solutions to possible edge cases and user pain points, researching and making competitive audits to further the design process, conducting usability studies and gathering actionable insights to iterate upon previous designs.
